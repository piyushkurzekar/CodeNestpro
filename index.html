FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to deliver a project?+
Most projects are delivered within 3–7 days depending on complexity. Simple websites or mini-projects can be done in 1–2 days. Complex AI systems may take 7–14 days. We always confirm the timeline before starting.
Can you help with academic final year projects?+
Yes! We specialize in final year and mini projects for B.Tech, MCA, BCA, MBA, and other programs. We provide full source code, project documentation, PPT preparation, and viva question guidance.
Do you provide source code and documentation?+
Absolutely. Every project includes complete source code, a README, setup instructions, and relevant documentation. For academic projects, we also provide formatted project reports and presentations.
What technologies do you work with?+
We work with Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV, LangChain, OpenAI API, React, Node.js, MongoDB, MySQL, FastAPI, Flask, React Native, and more. We choose the best stack for your specific project needs.
How do I get started?+
Simply fill in the contact form below or message us directly on WhatsApp. Describe your project, share your deadline, and we'll get back to you with a plan and quote within a few hours.
Do you offer revisions after delivery?+
Yes. We offer revisions within the agreed scope at no extra charge. If you need additional features beyond the original scope, we'll discuss them separately with a clear quote.
